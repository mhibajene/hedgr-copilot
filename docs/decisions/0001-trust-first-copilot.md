# ADR 0001 – Trust-first Copilot Posture

## Status
Accepted

## Date
2026-01-28

## Decision Type
UX/Trust

## Strategic Horizon
Long-Term (Moat)

## Reversibility
Reversible

---

## Context / Problem

Copilot risks being perceived as a “money printer” and increases liability if it nudges deposits/actions.

---

## Decision

Copilot is advisory-only, trust-first, and may recommend waiting / doing nothing.

---

## Rationale

- Trust is the Moat
- Cautious guidance aligns with Hedgrs "Protect + grow" positioning without hype. 

---

## Assumptions

- Users value credibility over excitement, especially in volatile economies.

---

## Risks / Trade-offs

- Slower activation/conversion vs more aggressive copilots.

## Revisit / Kill Criteria

- If trust-first materially harms adoption with no retention or trust signal upside.
