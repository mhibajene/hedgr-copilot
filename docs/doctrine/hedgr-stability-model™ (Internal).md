# Hedgr Stability Model™ (Internal)

Version: 1.0
Status: Binding – Internal Only
Classification: Confidential

---

## Purpose

The Hedgr Stability Model™ defines the rule-based framework governing capital allocation, liquidity protection, volatility response, and risk oversight within the Hedgr Stability Engine.

It operationalizes the Constitutional Charter and enforces Capital Preservation Above All.

This document defines mechanics. Public communications reference principles only.

---

# I. Core Objective

To preserve principal and deliver predictable capital outcomes across volatile market conditions.

Yield is secondary.
Liquidity is primary.
Risk is managed structurally, not reactively.

---

# II. Structural Allocation Framework

## A. Mandatory Dual Allocation (Core Offering)

All retail capital is automatically allocated into:

1. Stability Buffer
2. Conservative Yield Allocation

This split is system-managed.

User override is not permitted in the core offering.

---

## B. Stability Buffer (Internal Parameters)

The Stability Buffer:

- Maintains minimum threshold % of total capital.
- Is adjusted upward during stress.
- Prioritizes withdrawal liquidity.
- Avoids experimental or high-variance exposure.
- Is never fully deployed.

Internal controls include:

- Absolute minimum threshold (confidential %)
- Stress-mode elevated threshold (confidential %)
- Liquidity coverage ratio requirement
- Buffer protection during rebalancing

---

## C. Conservative Yield Allocation

Yield allocation:

- Is capped per protocol (confidential % limits)
- Is diversified across vetted protocols
- Is interruptible at any time
- Is reduced automatically under stress triggers
- May be suspended if principal risk increases

No leverage permitted in core allocation.

---

# III. Liquidity Priority Protocol

Liquidity is sacred.

The Stability Model enforces:

- Withdrawal-first design
- Liquidity coverage requirements
- Reallocation priority toward buffer under volatility
- Yield suspension authority when withdrawal risk rises
- Manual override authority for emergency scenarios (logged)

Withdrawal failure = Severity 1 event.

---

# IV. Volatility Response Mechanism

The Model includes predefined trigger categories:

- Stablecoin instability
- Protocol insolvency risk
- Liquidity surge events
- Regulatory intervention risk
- Counterparty exposure risk
- Oracle / pricing failure risk

Each trigger maps to:

- Buffer increase %
- Yield reduction %
- Exposure rebalancing rules
- Communication protocol activation
- Copilot tone downgrade

Triggers and thresholds remain internal.

---

# V. Risk Visibility Standard

The Model requires:

- Allocation transparency
- Buffer visibility
- Exposure distribution availability
- Risk tier classification per allocation
- Variance contextualization

Risk cannot be hidden in interface or communications.

---

# VI. Governance Enforcement

All structural changes to:

- Buffer minimums
- Exposure caps
- Trigger thresholds
- Yield deployment logic

Require:

- ADR entry
- Risk classification
- Liquidity impact analysis
- Charter alignment review
- HedgrOps approval

Tier 3 Doctrine Breaches are blocked unless formally overridden.

Override events must include:

- Written justification
- Risk acknowledgment
- Kill criteria
- Logged audit record

---

# VII. Crisis Mode Protocol

When stress conditions are met:

- Stability Buffer is elevated.
- Yield allocation may be reduced or suspended.
- Interface enters Calm Mode.
- Communications are transparent and measured.
- No urgency or defensive framing is permitted.

Crisis logic is predefined, not improvised.

---

# VIII. Model Evolution

The Hedgr Stability Model™ may evolve through:

- Governance-approved amendments
- Risk recalibration
- Macro condition reassessment
- Infrastructure upgrades

Amendments must not weaken Capital Preservation Doctrine.

All version changes must be logged.

---

# Closing Principle

The Hedgr Stability Model™ exists to reduce variance, preserve capital, and deliver predictable outcomes.

It is rule-driven.
It is auditable.
It is conservative by design.
