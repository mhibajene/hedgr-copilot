## 8. Trust UX Risk Notes

Any later user-facing language must avoid implying that the Philippines is selected, easier, supported, approved, available, lower-risk, faster, final, guaranteed, or Class B-ready.

Trust UX risks exposed by this classification:

- "Philippines supported" could be misread as PHP payout availability.
- "E-wallet / bank payout" could be misread as endpoint approval or provider confirmation.
- "InstaPay / PESONet" could be misread as rail approval for Hedgr's exact path.
- "Regulated VASP" could be misread as Hedgr legal availability or provider selection.
- "Completed" could be misread as local account credit or final settlement.
- "Low fee" or "known fee" could omit provider fees, FX spread, local endpoint charges, or changed final amount.
- "Manual review" could obscure unresolved support ownership and complaint duties.

Future copy should preserve visible uncertainty around endpoint eligibility, provider role, fee / FX, delay, failure, refund, support, complaint ownership, and incident stop conditions.
