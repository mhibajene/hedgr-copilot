## 7. Proposed counsel questions

The questions below are proposed scoping questions for a future separately authorized counsel engagement. They are not a legal memo request, formal opinion request, counsel instruction set, or legal reliance request.

### 7.1 Role boundary and non-custodial posture

- Can Hedgr maintain a non-custodial posture if it presents a provider-of-record same-name M-Pesa withdrawal path?
- What facts are required to assess Hedgr's role boundary across user flow, custody, routing, data, support, refund, cancellation, failed payout, and settlement truth?
- Which Hedgr actions, copy, status labels, controls, or support behaviors could cause Hedgr to appear to control funds, direct payouts, or own user outcomes?

### 7.2 Provider-of-record model

- What must be true for Hedgr to rely on a provider-of-record model?
- What facts are needed before provider-of-record responsibility can be legally relied on?
- Which responsibilities must be confirmed for conversion, fiat payout, local liquidity, failed payout handling, refunds, compliance, support, complaints, and settlement truth?
- What provider facts would counsel need before assessing whether Hedgr can describe the provider as responsible for the local rail?

### 7.3 Kenya jurisdictional perimeter

- What Kenya jurisdictional issues are raised by the same-name Kenya / KES / M-Pesa withdrawal hypothesis?
- What Kenya-specific facts would counsel need before considering VASP, digital-asset, payment, remittance, mobile-money, privacy, complaint, disclosure, or consumer-protection perimeter questions?
- What user-class distinctions matter for freelancers versus SME owners?

### 7.4 VASP / stablecoin / conversion perimeter

- What VASP, digital-asset, stablecoin, custody, reserve, redemption, disclosure, conversion, and settlement issues are raised by a stablecoin-originated or stablecoin-adjacent KES withdrawal path?
- Would the answer differ depending on chain, stablecoin, conversion owner, quote model, custody boundary, or provider entity?
- What facts are required before Hedgr can assess whether stablecoin-to-KES conversion changes Hedgr's legal role?

### 7.5 Payment / remittance / mobile-money perimeter

- What payment, remittance, mobile-money, e-money, payout, local-rail, MNO, or consumer-payment issues may arise from a same-name KES M-Pesa payout path?
- Does excluding third-party beneficiaries, payroll, supplier payouts, bulk disbursements, and remittance beneficiaries materially affect the perimeter?
- What must be true before M-Pesa endpoint support can be described safely?

### 7.6 KYC / KYB / AML / sanctions allocation

- What KYC, KYB, AML, sanctions, PEP, source-of-funds, and transaction-monitoring duties may arise for Hedgr, the provider, local partners, the MNO, and users?
- What facts are needed before responsibility can be allocated?
- What user-class differences matter for freelancer, SME owner, personal wallet, business wallet, beneficial ownership, and source-of-funds review?

### 7.7 Privacy / data-sharing / audit-retention

- What data-sharing, privacy, consent, notice, minimization, retention, audit, and cross-party data-transfer obligations may arise?
- What identity, phone, wallet, transaction, compliance, support, complaint, and audit data may need to be processed or shared?
- What retention and audit records would be needed before any later operational reliance?

### 7.8 Complaint / dispute / consumer protection

- What complaint, dispute, error-resolution, refund, timing, fee, finality, support, and consumer-protection issues are raised?
- Which party would need to own complaints for failed payout, delayed payout, wrong endpoint, changed amount, blocked payout, refund, cancellation, timeout, local wallet non-credit, suspected fraud, or compliance hold?
- What facts are required before Hedgr can describe support or complaint ownership to users?

### 7.9 Trust UX / disclosure language

- What user-facing claims would be unsafe without legal review, provider confirmation, and operational evidence?
- Which words or statements could overstate Kenya support, M-Pesa availability, withdrawal certainty, instant settlement, finality, fee certainty, refund certainty, provider compliance coverage, or local wallet credit?
- What minimum disclosure topics would counsel expect to review before any user-facing description of this path?

### 7.10 Hedgr-managed prefunding no-go / defer signal

- Would Hedgr-managed MNO, bank, PSP, agent, or local liquidity prefunding change the legal, custody, payment, treasury, liquidity, or customer-money perimeter?
- Would Hedgr-managed prefunding undermine the current non-custodial / provider-of-record thesis?
- What facts would counsel need to determine whether a path requiring Hedgr-managed prefunding should remain no-go / defer under current MVP posture?
