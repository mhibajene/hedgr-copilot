# HEDGR - Class B Legal / Compliance Evidence Package

## 1. Status / Authority / Scope

**Status:** Evidence package skeleton; documentation-only.

**Authority:** Non-authoritative evidence-planning artifact subordinate to `docs/ops/HEDGR_STATUS.md`, `AGENTS.md`, accepted ADRs, doctrine, `EVID-B-001`, `EVID-B-002`, and future qualified legal / compliance review.

**Scope:** Defines required legal / compliance evidence items, source expectations, review questions, blockers, freshness expectations, and downstream dependencies for future Class B consideration.

**Mode:** Evidence package scaffolding only; not evidence gathering, evidence acceptance, legal advice, regulatory approval, or Class B readiness.

This package does not provide legal advice, does not claim regulatory approval, does not approve a jurisdiction, customer eligibility, KYC / AML sufficiency, licensing posture, disclosures, privacy / retention posture, external engagement, Class B execution, implementation, staging, live operations, deposits, withdrawals, custody, rails, stablecoin conversion, treasury operations, Copilot execution, or customer fund movement.

## 2. Purpose

This package exists to instantiate the legal / compliance evidence package from the evidence gathering plan and registry.

It answers:

**What legal / compliance evidence must be gathered and reviewed before future Class B ADR drafting readiness or implementation-proposal preflight can be considered?**

It does not answer:

**Is Hedgr legally permitted or approved to operate a Class B pilot?**

It does not create:

- legal approval
- compliance approval
- jurisdiction selection
- customer eligibility approval
- KYC / AML approval
- licensing determination
- disclosure approval
- external legal engagement approval
- implementation authority
- customer-money authority

## 6. Open legal / compliance questions

Answers must come from qualified legal / compliance review and future governed acceptance, not this package.

### Jurisdiction and regulatory perimeter

- [ ] Which jurisdiction is the first Class B pilot being assessed for?
- [ ] Where is Hedgr operating from?
- [ ] Where are users located?
- [ ] Which legal role would Hedgr play?
- [ ] Would the contemplated activity be regulated?
- [ ] What licenses, registrations, exemptions, sandbox approvals, partner arrangements, or agent structures may be required?

### Customer eligibility

- [ ] Who may participate in a future pilot?
- [ ] Are participants internal testers, invited users, individuals, SMEs, employees, or external customers?
- [ ] What residency, sanctions, age, KYC, business-type, transaction-limit, or exposure-limit constraints apply?

### KYC / AML / sanctions

- [ ] Who performs KYC?
- [ ] Who performs AML monitoring?
- [ ] Who screens sanctions and PEPs?
- [ ] What happens when onboarding fails, flags, or requires manual review?
- [ ] What evidence must be retained?

### Custody and funds control

- [ ] Who legally controls or custodies user assets?
- [ ] What custody representations are allowed?
- [ ] What recovery, access, protection, and loss claims are prohibited?
- [ ] What provider responsibilities must be reviewed?

### Rails, payments, and settlement

- [ ] Which deposit or withdrawal rails are contemplated?
- [ ] Who is the sender, recipient, collector, merchant, agent, or settlement party?
- [ ] What permissions are required for each rail?
- [ ] What settlement / finality claims are legally safe?

### Stablecoin, FX, fees, and conversion

- [ ] Would the pilot involve stablecoin acquisition, holding, conversion, redemption, or transfer?
- [ ] What stablecoin risk disclosures are required?
- [ ] What FX, fee, spread, slippage, and settlement timing disclosures are required?
- [ ] What claims around rates or timing are prohibited?

### Disclosures, marketing, support, and complaints

- [ ] What must users be told before any customer-money action?
- [ ] What public claims are prohibited?
- [ ] What support, complaint, dispute, and incident handling obligations apply?
- [ ] What user communication timelines or constraints apply?

### Privacy, retention, and audit

- [ ] What user data is collected?
- [ ] Where is it stored?
- [ ] Who can access it?
- [ ] How long must it be retained?
- [ ] What audit records must exist for Class B review?
